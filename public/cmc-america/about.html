Our History of Mixers

Industrial Dough Mixers and Bakery Equipment, built in Joliet, Illinois since 1888.

Est. 1888

A company that serves the baking industry throughout the globe.

CMC-America is located in Joliet, IL USA. It was here, in 1888 that Mr. Phillip F. Carroll started to manufacture bakery equipment for the rapidly growing retail baking industry. What Mr. Carroll first started continues today as CMC America, a company that serves the baking industry throughout the globe.

We hope you will enjoy the presentation of achievements and select moments that the company has celebrated through the years. As our most recent patent from 2011 proves, we are a forward-looking company. But, we are as proud of those who came before us as we are of where we are headed.
